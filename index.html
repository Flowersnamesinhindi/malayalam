Flowers Name in Hindi

Learn about various beautiful flowers and their names in Hindi

Introduction

Flowers are an integral part of nature, not only enhancing the beauty of the environment but also carrying cultural and emotional significance. In India, flowers are used in various rituals, celebrations, and festivals. Here, we will explore the names of popular flowers in Hindi.

List of Flowers Name in Hindi

  • Rose - गुलाब (Gulab)
  • Lily - कुमुद (Kumud)
  • Lotus - कमल (Kamal)
  • Sunflower - सूरजमुखी (Surajmukhi)
  • Tulip - ट्यूलिप (Tulip)
  • Marigold - गेंदा (Genda)
  • Jasmine - चमेली (Chameli)
  • Daisy - गुलबहार (Gulbahar)
  • Orchid - ऑर्किड (Orchid)
  • Champa - चंपा (Champa)
  • Hibiscus - गुड़हल (Gudhhal)
  • Chrysanthemum - गुलदाउदी (Guldaudi)
  • Plumeria - फ्रांगिपानी (Frangipani)
  • Ixora - जवंती (Javanti)
  • Poppy - अफीम (Afeem)
  • Lavender - लैवेंडर (Lavender)
  • Magnolia - मोलग्नोलिया (Magnolia)

These are just a few examples of the many flowers found in India. Flowers play an essential role in our culture, symbolizing beauty, love, peace, and purity.
